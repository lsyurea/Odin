Making authentic German Potato Salad

Ingredients

    3 cups diced peeled potatoes
    4 slices bacon
    1 small onion, diced
    1/4 cup white vinegar
    2 tablespoons water
    3 tablespoons white sugar
    1 teaspooon salt
    1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
    1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
